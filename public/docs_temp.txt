      <section class="card" id="intro">
        <h2>How To Use This App</h2>
        <p class="muted">This guide explains each chart/table, how to interact with it, and how to interpret results for better trading decisions.</p>
      </section>
      <section class="card" id="total-chart">
        <h2>Total USDT Balance Over Time</h2>
        <p>Plots combined Total USDT: DEX (usdtVal + coinVal) plus BinanceF (USDT wallet + sum of unrealized PnL). Use mouse wheel or drag to zoom, pinch on trackpad, and hold Ctrl + drag to pan. Click Reset Zoom in the header to return to the full range. Toggle series in the legend to isolate trends.</p>
        <p><strong>Why it matters:</strong> Reveals equity drift; step changes often indicate deposits/withdrawals or structural PnL changes.</p>
      </section>
      <section class="card" id="dex-table">
        <h2>DEX Exchange Balances</h2>
        <p>Breaks down DEX balances by exchange and token (only tokens with totalUsdt &gt; 0.1). Use the search box and click table headers to sort. The “Total DEX USDT” summary aggregates usdtVal and coinVal across exchanges.</p>
      </section>
      <section class="card" id="cex-table">
        <h2>BinanceF Balances</h2>
        <p>Shows USDT total as wallet USDT plus sum of unrealized PnL from positions. Token USDT value formula: (entryPrice × total)/leverage + unrealizedProfit. Sort and search like other tables.</p>
      </section>
      <section class="card" id="trades-table">
        <h2>Completed Trades</h2>
        <p>Includes pair, executedGrossProfit (green positive, red negative), Quantity (executedSrcPrice × executedQtySrc), timestamps, and parsed props (Dex, Diff, DexSlip, CexSlip). Click headers to sort and use search to filter.</p>
      </section>
      <section class="card" id="bar-total-profit">
        <h2>Total Gross Profit by Pair</h2>
        <p>Bar chart of top pairs by total gross profit. Quickly highlights where the system makes most of its money.</p>
      </section>
      <section class="card" id="winners-rate">
        <h2>Win Rate (Top Winners)</h2>
        <p>Win% for top profitable pairs. Use alongside total profit to assess risk-adjusted consistency.</p>
      </section>
      <section class="card" id="losers-loss">
        <h2>Total Loss (Top Losers)</h2>
        <p>Absolute total losses by pair. Helps prioritize mitigation (tighter limits, hedging, or disabling pairs).</p>
      </section>
      <section class="card" id="features-table">
        <h2>Pair Feature Differences</h2>
        <p>All pairs with trades count, total profit (colored), and average feature values for wins vs losses: Diff, DexSlip, CexSlip, plus overall CexSlip average. Click columns to sort; search to filter.</p>
        <p><strong>Reading CexSlip:</strong> Positive values (red) suggest headwinds from CEX slippage; negative values (green) indicate favorable conditions.</p>
      </section>
      <section class="card" id="cum-gp">
        <h2>Cumulative Gross Profit (Deep Dive)</h2>
        <p>Pair-wise cumulative sum of executedGrossProfit. Use zoom to examine regimes. A smooth upward line indicates stable profitability.</p>
      </section>
      <section class="card" id="hist-gp">
        <h2>Gross Profit Distribution (Deep Dive)</h2>
        <p>Histogram of gross profit values (or selected metric). Look for skew, tails, and outliers; thin tails and positive skew are healthy.</p>
      </section>
      <section class="card" id="scatter-deep">
        <h2>Feature vs Profit (Deep Dive)</h2>
        <p>Scatter of chosen X vs gross profit. Nonlinear shapes suggest the need for thresholds or different sizing rules.</p>
      </section>
