How To Use This App

This guide explains each chart/table, how to interact with it, and how to interpret results for better trading decisions.

Total USDT Balance Over Time

Plots combined Total USDT: DEX (usdtVal + coinVal) plus BinanceF (USDT wallet + sum of unrealized PnL). Use mouse wheel or drag to zoom, pinch on trackpad, and hold Ctrl + drag to pan. Click Reset Zoom in the header to return to the full range. Toggle series in the legend to isolate trends.

Why it matters: Reveals equity drift; step changes often indicate deposits/withdrawals or structural PnL changes.

DEX Exchange Balances

Breaks down DEX balances by exchange and token (only tokens with totalUsdt > 0.1). Use the search box and click table headers to sort. The “Total DEX USDT” summary aggregates usdtVal and coinVal across exchanges.

BinanceF Balances

Shows USDT total as wallet USDT plus sum of unrealized PnL from positions. Token USDT value formula: (entryPrice × total)/leverage + unrealizedProfit. Sort and search like other tables.

Completed Trades

Includes pair, executedGrossProfit (green positive, red negative), Quantity (executedSrcPrice × executedQtySrc), timestamps, and parsed props (Dex, Diff, DexSlip, CexSlip). Click headers to sort and use search to filter.

Total Gross Profit by Pair

Bar chart of top pairs by total gross profit. Quickly highlights where the system makes most of its money.

Win Rate (Top Winners)

Win% for top profitable pairs. Use alongside total profit to assess risk-adjusted consistency.

Total Loss (Top Losers)

Absolute total losses by pair. Helps prioritize mitigation (tighter limits, hedging, or disabling pairs).

Pair Feature Differences

All pairs with trades count, total profit (colored), and average feature values for wins vs losses: Diff, DexSlip, CexSlip, plus overall CexSlip average. Click columns to sort; search to filter.

Reading CexSlip: Positive values (red) suggest headwinds from CEX slippage; negative values (green) indicate favorable conditions.

Cumulative Gross Profit (Deep Dive)

Pair-wise cumulative sum of executedGrossProfit. Use zoom to examine regimes. A smooth upward line indicates stable profitability.

Gross Profit Distribution (Deep Dive)

Histogram of gross profit values (or selected metric). Look for skew, tails, and outliers; thin tails and positive skew are healthy.

Feature vs Profit (Deep Dive)

Scatter of chosen X vs gross profit. Nonlinear shapes suggest the need for thresholds or different sizing rules.

Scatter + Regression (ML Analysis)

Linear fit (slope, intercept), correlation, and R². Points colored by sign of Y. Use Clip Outliers to tame extremes before inspecting fit.

Histogram X (ML Analysis)

Distribution of the selected X variable. Use Bins to change resolution.

Histogram Y (ML Analysis)

Distribution of Y. Apply Clip Outliers (%) to focus on the main mass of data.

Residuals (ML Analysis)

Residual = Y − Ŷ. Pattern-free cloud around zero indicates a decent linear model. Structure suggests nonlinearity or missing features.

Correlation Matrix (ML Analysis)

Select variables and compute Pearson correlations. Green = positive, Red = negative; intensity ~ |corr|. Use to screen features and detect collinearity.
